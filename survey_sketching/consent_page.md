# Consent
Thank you for considering participation in this study! Below are key details to ensure informed consent:  

## Purpose
This study aims to explore how autonomous vehicles (AVs) communicate with pedestrians. Your input will help us better understand and improve these interactions.

## Duration
The average duration of the study is 10 minutes. 

## Risks 
There are no known risks associated with participating in this study. 

## Confidentiality
Your privacy is our priority. We will not collect any personally identifiable information. The survey is hosted on Cognition.run and you can learn more about their data security [here](https://docs.cognition.run/data-management-and-download)

Please note that responses from this study may be shared publicly; however, all data will be anonymized to protect your identity.

## Your Rights  
Participation is entirely voluntary. You may decline or withdraw at any time, but your entitled benefits will be adjusted accordingly.  

## Note
The data collected will be used solely for research purposes and will not be used to train commercial AI models.

## Questions
If you have any questions about this research, please feel free to contact Ronald Cumbal at ronald.cumbal@it.uu.se.

By proceeding, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this information. Thank you for your valuable input!
